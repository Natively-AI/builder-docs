## Discord community (fastest)

For the quickest responses, join our Discord server:

**[Join Native.Builder on Discord](https://discord.gg/AqxSSUaHfX)**

Ask questions, share what you're building, get tips from other users, and hear about updates from the team. You can also open Discord from the sidebar inside the app.

<Note>
  Discord is best for how-to questions and community help. For bugs, billing issues, or account problems, use in-app Help or email so the team can track your request.
</Note>

## In-app help

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Help">
    Click **Help** in the sidebar (life buoy icon).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your intent">
    Select what you need help with:
    - **Report a bug** — Something broke or isn't working
    - **Ask a question** — How-to or product question
    - **Request a feature** — Something you'd like added to Builder
    - **I'm stuck** — You need guidance to move forward
  </Step>
  <Step title="Describe the issue">
    Provide details about what happened, what you expected, and any steps to reproduce the problem. You can attach a screenshot.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit">
    Your request goes directly to the team. You'll see a confirmation when it's received.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Email support

For account issues, billing questions, or anything you can't resolve in-app:

**[support@nativelyai.com](mailto:support@nativelyai.com)**

Include your workspace name, project ID (if relevant), and a description of the issue.
